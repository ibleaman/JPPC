### Chutzpah:

**Merkin Hall at Kaufman Music Center  
129 West 67th Street  
New York City**

*Ba di taykhn fun Bovl ~ Dona, Dona ~ Flaterl ~ Harbstlid ~  
Hatikvah in Yiddish ("Di hofenung") ~ In kamf ~ Der Internatsyonal ~
